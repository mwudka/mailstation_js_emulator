This javascript Mailstation emulator is a work (lacking) in progress. The "buttons" under the LCD do not work, and as you may be able to tell, I have not yet mastered style sheets. The mouse only works with the mailstation menubar, and as the menu buttons do not work, you have to use the mouse to make selection from menubar. Just click anywhere on LCD, either on the menubar itself, or the menu icons above it. You need to use the keyboard for everything else.
